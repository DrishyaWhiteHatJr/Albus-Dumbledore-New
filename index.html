Albus Dumbledore

About

Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore was so much more than just the headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was just like a grandfather to Harry and guided all of us throught some of the darkest times in the Wizarding World. He was a kind, powerful, mysterious and wise person. He never hesitated to sacrifise for all his students and even strangers.

Happiness can be found in even the darkest of of times, if only one remembers to turn on the light. -Albus Dumbledore
